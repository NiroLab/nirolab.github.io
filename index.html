About NIRO Lab

The NSU Intelligent Robotics Lab (NIRO Lab) is a cutting-edge research facility at North South University dedicated to advancing the frontiers of robotics, artificial intelligence, and autonomous systems. Our multidisciplinary team of researchers, faculty, and students work collaboratively on innovative projects that address real-world challenges in areas such as computer vision, machine learning, human-robot interaction, and autonomous navigation.
