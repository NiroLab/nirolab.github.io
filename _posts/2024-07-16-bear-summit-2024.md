The NSU Intelligent Robotics Lab (NIRO Lab) participated in the **BEAR Summit 2024**, held on July 16-17, 2024 in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The event, themed "Bangladesh as a Nation of Innovation," brought together researchers and industry leaders to showcase advancements in biotechnology, engineering, AI, and robotics.

## A Platform for Innovation

The summit served as a crucial platform to display innovations and connect academia with industry partners. NIRO Lab demonstrated its commitment to contributing to Bangladesh's technology ecosystem through our ongoing research in robotics and artificial intelligence.

## Our Mission

NIRO Lab focuses on:
- Advancing robotics research
- Educational excellence
- Industrial collaboration

The event emphasized Bangladesh's growing reputation in science and technology globally, and NIRO Lab aims to position the nation as a technology-driven leader.

## Looking Forward

The summit represented a broader movement toward establishing Bangladesh as an innovation hub on the global stage. We are proud to be part of this journey and look forward to continuing our contributions to the field of intelligent robotics.
