# NIRO EDU BOT Secures Top 3 Position in Robotics Segment at BEAR Summit 2025
![NIRO EDU BOT at BEAR Summit 2025](NIROBEARCREST.jpg)

The **NSU Intelligent Robotics Lab (NIRO)** achieved a notable accomplishment as its project, **NIRO EDU BOT**, secured a **Top 3 position** in the **Robotics** segment at **BEAR Summit 2025**, held in **Dhaka** in **July 2025**.

**BEAR Summit 2025** was Bangladesh’s first major national technology summit focused on **Biotech, Electronics, AI, and Robotics**. Organized by the **ICT Division** and the **Bangladesh Computer Council (BCC)**, the event brought together researchers, innovators, industry experts, and academic institutions to showcase advancements across emerging technology sectors.

![NIRO EDU BOT at BEAR Summit 2025](NIROATBEARSUMMIT.jpg)

The achievement reflects the sustained efforts of the NIRO Lab team under the supervision of **Dr. Shahnewaz Siddique**. The recognition at BEAR Summit 2025 highlights NIRO Lab’s ongoing commitment to robotics education, research, and innovation, as well as its contribution to the growing technology ecosystem in Bangladesh.
