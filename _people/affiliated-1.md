Dr. Md Shahriar Karim is an Associate Professor and Graduate Coordinator in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at North South University.

## Education

- **PhD** in Biological Engineering (Computational Life Science), Purdue University, USA (2016)
- **MS** in Electrical and Computer Engineering, Purdue University, USA
- **MS** in Biological Engineering, Purdue University, USA
- **B.Tech** in Electronics Engineering, NIT Allahabad, India

## Research Focus

Dr. Karim's research interests include Quantitative Systems Biology, modeling of dynamical systems, estimation and detection theory, and computational biology applications. His work spans Artificial Intelligence & Robotics, Modeling and Simulation, Bioinformatics and Biotechnology, and Signals and Image Processing.

## Teaching

Dr. Karim teaches courses in discrete mathematics, numerical methods, programming languages, senior design projects, and modeling/simulation at both undergraduate and graduate levels.
