Dr. Riasat Khan is an Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at North South University.

## Education

- **PhD** in Electrical and Computer Engineering, New Mexico State University, USA
- **MSc** in Electrical and Computer Engineering, New Mexico State University, USA
- **BSc** in Electrical & Electronic Engineering, Islamic University of Technology, Bangladesh

## Research Focus

Dr. Khan's research interests include Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Smart Systems and IoT. His work spans Artificial Intelligence & Robotics, Modeling and Simulation, and Embedded Systems and Internet of Things (IoT).

## Professional Involvement

Dr. Khan serves as a BAETE Evaluator (Tier-1) and Faculty Advisor for IEEE Industry Applications Society. He previously served as Undergraduate Program Coordinator (EEE/ETE) at NSU from 2023-2024 and received the Outstanding Teaching Assistant Award during his doctoral studies.
