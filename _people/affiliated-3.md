Dr. Mohammad Abdul Qayum is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at North South University.

## Education

- **PhD** in Electrical Engineering (Computer Engineering), New Mexico State University, USA
- **Post-doctoral Fellowship**, New Mexico State University, USA
- **MSc** in Electrical Engineering (Computer Engineering), Oklahoma State University, USA
- **BSc** in Electrical & Electronic Engineering, BUET, Bangladesh

## Research Focus

Dr. Qayum's research interests include High Performance Computing, Internet of Things (IoT), Robotics, and Blockchains. His current projects involve Urban Heat Island solutions and robot dexterity intelligence using reinforcement learning with blockchain capabilities.

## Professional Experience

Dr. Qayum has held positions at Minnesota State University where he taught microprocessor and embedded systems courses, and has experience as a developer and investor in blockchain startups.
